The Journey

How I found my voice

The Beginning
Where My Passion Began
In March 2020, my third grade year suddenly moved online. Class was no longer inside a classroom but through a screen. I couldn’t see my friends every day or share stories the way I used to. With more free time and fewer ways to interact, I started writing short fictional stories just to pass the time. Writing became a way to create my own world and express myself in a new way.
Discovering Storytelling
Falling in love with narratives
I started exploring different forms of writing and quickly fell in love with narratives. Talking to people and sharing fun, interesting stories has always been one of my favorite things to do. Writing allowed me to capture those moments, relive them, and share them with even more people.
Growing as a writer
Exploring issues that matter
Although I loved writing personal essays, I wanted to grow as a writer. I began researching topics that interested me, such as air pollution and electric vehicles. I wanted to make an impact by informing others about important issues.
Now
Continuing to grow and share
I want to continue sharing my writing with others. I hope to write more about important events happening today that interest both me and others. I will also be extending my knowledge of writing by interviewing people with STEM backgrounds and writing articles about them. I hope you follow my journey as a writer!
